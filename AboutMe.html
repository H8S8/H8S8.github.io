About Me

How I got here, and where I want to go next

I am a recent BSc Physics graduate from the University of Manchester. I am looking to start a career in tech.

Why I want to start a career in tech

I am an avid problem solver, I can never resist having a go at a difficult puzzle. During my second year at university, I found that the modules I was enjoying the most all involved programming or data analysis. Since discovering this, I have endeavoured to broaden my skills by taking every programming course offered in my university program, joining the robotics society at my university, and taking online courses in my own time. By building a career in the technology industry, I hope to a continue to expand my coding knowledge and collaborate on projects that I find rewarding.
